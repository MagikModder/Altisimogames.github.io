Welcome to DayZ.

DayZ is an open world survival based game. So we made a mod off of it. again. An unknown infection has wiped out most of the world's population. Go Solo or team up with friends to take on the world as you choose your path in this brutal and chilling landscape using whatever means you stumble upon to survive.

The Features.

DayZ Mod includes everything from the original but with more. There are over 10 new guns, Vehicles, New armor, New Animations. Its so much better, you would barely recognize it as the same mod.
